Tips & Tricks for Success

  • • Start by mastering individual tasks before trying to juggle multiple ones.
  • • Take short breaks between sessions to keep your mind fresh and focused.
  • • Practice rhythmic breathing to maintain calm during intense multitasking moments.
  • • Try to develop a scanning pattern that lets you quickly check the status of all tasks.
  • • Challenge friends to beat your score for extra motivation and fun competition.
